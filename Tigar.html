Tiger

The tiger is one of the most famous and powerful big cats. It belongs to the cat family like the lion and is known for its strong, flexible body and distinctive coat with black stripes on an orange or golden background, which helps it camouflage while hunting. Tigers usually live in dense forests and grasslands and are nocturnal predators that hunt alone. Their diet mainly includes deer, wild boars, monkeys, and sometimes smaller animals. Tigers are symbols of strength and courage in many cultures, but they are now threatened by habitat loss and poaching.
